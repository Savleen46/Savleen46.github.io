Departments

Enver Creek strives to improve the students performances in academics.

Here are some departments that we focus on.

Math

Enver Creek's Mathematics are divided into three categories for different levels of career or post secondary preparation.

Math at the grade 12 level is optional

Science

We are passionate about encouraging and inspiring the next generaton of scientists, doctors, nurses and engineers.

We want to engage students creativity and innovation by offering open-ended challenges and more complex problems.

English

The English Department offers mandatory courses English 9-12.

The English curriculum offers studies of literature in the areas of poetry, short stories, novels, and drama.
